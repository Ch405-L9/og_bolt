# RSVP_ORP Reader - Compliance & Privacy Documentation

## Privacy Policy

### Data Collection & Usage

**RSVP_ORP Reader** ("the App") is committed to protecting user privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

#### Information We Collect

1. **Reading Data**
   - Book titles and metadata
   - Reading progress and position
   - Reading speed preferences
   - Bookmarks and notes
   - Session duration and frequency

2. **Device Information**
   - Device model and OS version
   - App version
   - Crash reports and error logs

3. **User Preferences**
   - Display settings (dark mode, text size)
   - Speed preferences
   - Comprehension mode settings

#### How We Use Your Information

- To provide and improve the reading experience
- To track reading progress and statistics
- To generate personalized reading insights
- To fix bugs and improve app stability
- To comply with legal obligations

#### Data Storage

- All reading data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- No data is transmitted to external servers without explicit user consent
- Users have full control over their data and can delete it at any time

#### Third-Party Services

The App does not share personal data with third parties except:
- As required by law
- With user explicit consent
- For essential service providers (with strict confidentiality agreements)

#### Data Retention

- Reading data is retained as long as the app is installed
- Users can delete individual books or clear all data from settings
- Uninstalling the app removes all associated data

### User Rights

Users have the right to:
- Access their personal data
- Request data deletion
- Opt-out of analytics
- Control app permissions

## Google Play Store Compliance

### Required Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

1. **READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE** - To import books from device storage
2. **WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE** - To save reading progress and bookmarks
3. **INTERNET** - For future cloud sync features (optional)
